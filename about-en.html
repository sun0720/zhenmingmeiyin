About Us

Professional manufacturer of food and beverage flexible packaging solutions

ZM PACK

Founded in 2003, ZM PACK is located in Meilong Industrial Park, Anbu Town, Chaoan County, Chaozhou City, Guangdong, China. Qualified with high standard clean workshop, advanced equipment and scientific quality management system, we specialize in the production of food and beverage soft packing with different shapes and materials (such as BOPP, BOPA, PET/MPET, PE, LDPE, MCP and PVC tangled, pearlized and matt film). Providing customers with delicate and qualified packaging products, we are in the persistent pursuit of "creating the first-class products with a good reputation and excellent services".

Sincerely welcome you to visit our company. It will be our greatest pleasure to receive your inquiry.
